Omelette

Omelette is a simple receipe that can be made using eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Salt
  • Coriander
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Add Butter in the pan and let it heat.
  2. Once the butter is heated nicely, Add break and add the eggs.
  3. Add Salt and chilli flakes as per taste
  4. Let it cook.
  5. Finally add some coriander leaves.
  6. Once ready stop the heat and remove and place the omelette in your plate.
